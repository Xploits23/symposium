Technical Events
Paper Presentation
  • Papers should be sent on Standard Format to the mail ID : xploits2023@gmail.com, Presentation can be done as a team or as an individual. The abstracts will be displayed on the magazine.
    • Switch 'N' Code
  • Coding round take place as two phases. A team of two members will participate. One will start the coding and does upto a scheduled time and the next half will be done by the other team member. Co-operation and understanding between the two coders and the knowledge in domain will be tested in this event.
    • Catch the Code
  • Catch the code is an treasure hunt based on coding. A code with errors will be provided and the team will be given time to debug and find the output. The output has an hint about the treasure. The treasure has an question in it. The team which finds the treasure and give the right answer will be the winner.
    • Digithon
  • As an individual or as a team of maximum 4 can participate in this event. A topic will be allocated to the team and the team is expected to create a digital world. Personal system can be bought too for the competition.
    • Non-Technical Events
    Adzap
  • A product will be given, and you're expected to market the given product. It can either be a one to one interaction or in the form of a drama (TV advertisement). Can participate as an individual or as an team.
    • Sherlock
  • It's an crime investigation event, the team is allowed to view the crime location for a particular time and asked to answer the questions. The most accurate answering team will be the winner.
    • Ship Wreck
  • The participants are given a topic and asked to talk about it. Whenever the panel members say "SWAP" they should change the between advantages and disadvantages of the topic.
